About the College

Bharata Mata College (Autonomous) Thrikkakara, established in 1965 under the Bharata Mata Educational Trust, is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University and managed by the Archdiocese of Ernakulam–Angamaly.

Located along the seaport–airport road in Thrikkakara, the college earned an A+ grade from NAAC (2019). Guided by the motto “For God and country”, Bharata Mata College provides a holistic education that blends academic excellence with ethical values and responsible citizenship.

  • Established: 1965
  • Affiliation: Mahatma Gandhi University
  • NAAC Grade: A+ (2019)
  • Focus: Holistic education & community outreach

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science, established in January 2021 within the Government-aided stream, has made significant strides with the launch of its Integrated MSc in Computer Science (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning). This program is designed to foster a generation of technologically adept youth who are not only proficient in technical skills but also embody responsible citizenship, ethical values, and empathy. The department’s vision emphasizes a commitment to excellence in education, equipping students with the tools to tackle contemporary technological challenges. By blending theoretical knowledge with practical application and ethical principles, the Integrated MSc program prepares graduates to contribute meaningfully to society.

About the Conference

The International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning aims to bring together industry leaders, researchers, and practitioners to explore the latest advancements in AI and Machine Learning. This three-day event features keynote speeches, panel discussions, and paper presentations — providing a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange and collaboration in the evolving landscape of AI and ML.

Call for Papers

Original research papers addressing themes relating to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Conference Objectives

  • Provide a platform for researchers to share knowledge and latest research in AI and ML
  • Promote interdisciplinary collaboration between academic research and industrial applications
  • Explore emerging trends in AI, ML, Deep Learning, Data Science, and Computer Vision
  • Encourage young researchers and students to engage in innovative projects
  • Facilitate networking and partnership opportunities among global experts
  • Showcase practical applications of AI and ML across various industries

Research Topics

Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning Deep Learning Data Science Computer Vision Natural Language Processing Reinforcement Learning Neural Networks

Abstract Format

A single paragraph of not more than 300 words, including the title of the paper, author name(s), institution/affiliation, and the email address of the corresponding author.
